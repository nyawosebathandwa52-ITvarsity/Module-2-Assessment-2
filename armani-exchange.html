ARMANI EXCHANGE


Created by the famous Italian fashion house of Giorgio Armani, AX Armani Exchange is the diffusion label with a modern, accessible aesthetic, Bringing together the hallmarks of the main Armani line, AX Armani Exchange presents easy-to-wear pieces that bring fuse Italian haute couture techniques with a sense of versatility. From classic blue jeans to skinny black jeans, the line of AX Armani Exchange jeans are the epitome of everyday, casual-cool styling and will become staples in your wardrobe for years to come.
